FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Everything you need to know about Kōda.

What is Kōda?
Kōda is an AI-powered trading journal for serious traders. It tracks your P&L, win rate, R-multiples, and discipline so you can find your edge — not just your entries.
Is my trade data private?
Yes. Your trade data is stored in your private Supabase database and is never shared or sold. Row-level security ensures only you can read your trades.
What's the difference between Free and Pro?
Free gives you unlimited manual trade logging, basic stats, and one circle. Pro adds auto-import via Tradovate, unlimited circles, the prop firm tracker, Kōda AI insights, and priority support.
How do I import trades from my broker?
Go to Home → Sync. You can connect Tradovate for live auto-import, or upload a CSV export from any platform — Kōda auto-detects the format.
Does Kōda support futures?
Yes. The Lot Size Calculator includes full specs for ES, NQ, MES, MNQ, CL, GC, and more. The journal tracks R-multiples and P&L for both futures and forex.
How do Circles work?
Circles are private groups where traders share journals, run challenges, and compare stats. You can join by code or discover public circles. Free users get one circle; Pro unlocks unlimited.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription?
Yes, any time from Settings → Billing. Your Pro access continues until the end of the billing period.
